# OpsProbe v0.8.2

Released: 2026-06-05

`v0.8.2` continues the `0.8.x` exploration line with more useful operator and manager reports for recurring inspection work.

## Highlights

- Adds an operator-facing `Action Queue` for abnormal items in priority order
- Adds a `Recurring Actions` section to surface repeated abnormal findings across included runs
- Adds shorter `Evidence signal` and `Action focus` summaries for faster report scanning

## Why this release exists

The deeper inspection work in `0.8.0` and `0.8.1` improved what OpsProbe can detect, but recurring operations work also depends on how quickly users can decide what to do next. `0.8.2` improves the report layer so repeated findings, next actions, and the most important evidence are easier to consume without reading every detail first.
